Welcome to Midway

Get to know our church family.

Tired of shallow, man-centered services? Midway Baptist Church is an independent church; we are not associated with any denominational association.

We are Fundamental and Baptist in our beliefs and practices. Our church services are Christ-centered, our preaching aims to exalt Christ and God's Word, our music is traditional and non-contemporary.

We invite you to join us as we worship together, study God's Word, and grow in faith as a community. Whether you're seeking answers, looking for a church home, or simply curious, you're welcome here.
